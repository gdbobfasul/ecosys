Privacy Policy

App package: com.kcy.routinebot.hw · Store: HUAWEI AppGallery · Provider: Dai Grup Ltd.

A personal daily assistant that runs a morning briefing (weather, agenda and motivation), fires reminders for medication, habits and tasks, and gives an optional evening summary. It can also read your notes aloud in any of 15 languages.

1. We do not collect personal data

This application has no user accounts, no registration and no login by us. We do not collect, store or transmit personal information such as your name, email, phone number, contacts or precise location. We do not use advertising or third-party analytics / tracking SDKs.

2. Data stored on your device

Your settings and app data (such as the interface language you choose, and any content the app keeps for you) are stored locally on your device. This data is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Device permissions

The app requests a permission only when a feature needs it:

  • Notifications — used only to show you local alerts from the app on your device.

4. Network

The app uses the internet for its stated functions. Network is used only to fetch weather from a public service by a city NAME the user types (no GPS/device location). Text-to-speech uses the device speaker. Reminders, notes and settings are stored locally; local notifications only, no account, no cloud.

5. Children

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this address.
