A price watcher that tracks selected crypto (BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, XRP, ADA, DOGE) and currency rates and sends a local notification the moment a price crosses a threshold you set. It only reads prices from free public sources and never handles payments or wallets.

A robot that watches prices for you

This robot watches selected crypto and currency rates and notifies you as soon as a price drops below or rises above the threshold you set.

Everything runs on the device. No account, no contacts, no tracking. Prices come from free public sources.
