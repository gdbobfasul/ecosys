Privacy Policy

App package: com.kcy.babymonitor.rustore · Store: RuStore · Provider: Dai Grup Ltd.

A camera-based awareness assistant that watches your sleeping or playing child through the phone camera and alerts you on motion: it flags stirring/waking, a second person appearing in the room, or the child leaving the frame. Detection runs on-device with a local notification, sound, and a photo log for each event.

1. We do not collect personal data

This application has no user accounts, no registration and no login by us. We do not collect, store or transmit personal information such as your name, email, phone number, contacts or precise location. We do not use advertising or third-party analytics / tracking SDKs.

2. Data stored on your device

Your settings and app data (such as the interface language you choose, and any content the app keeps for you) are stored locally on your device. This data is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Device permissions

The app requests a permission only when a feature needs it:

  • Camera — used only for the app's camera feature; the image is processed on your device and is not uploaded by us.
  • Notifications — used only to show you local alerts from the app on your device.

4. Network

The app uses the internet for its stated functions. Video is processed on the device and never uploaded; event snapshots and logs stay local. Network is used only for a one-time free AI-model download and optionally a user-supplied camera/relay URL. In-app disclaimer states it is NOT a certified safety device.

5. Children

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this address.

7. Contact
