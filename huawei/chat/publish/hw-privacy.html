Privacy Policy — Pupikes Chat

App: Pupikes Chat · Package: com.pupikes.chat.hw · Store: HUAWEI AppGallery · Provider (data controller): Dai Grup Ltd. · Effective date: 2026-07-12

Pupikes Chat is a real-time messaging app that connects you to the Pupikes chat service. Pick from 15 languages and start chatting once you are online. This app is a thin shell that loads our live online service at my.girl.place in a secure web view. Because it is an online service, some data you provide leaves your device and is stored on our server — this policy explains what and why. We do not sell any data and we do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

1. Data processed by our online service

To use Pupikes Chat you create an account / sign in and use the service. The following data is sent to and stored on our server (Dai Grup Ltd., my.girl.place): your account/registration details and the content you create in the service (the messages you send, and profile/service details you choose to add). We use it solely to operate the service for you. Messages are delivered to the people you chat with. Do not share more personal data than you are comfortable with.

Pupikes apps are like a faithful, trainable dog — you can improve them through the in-app feedback button, but each app helps only within the limits of its purpose.

2. Data stored on your device

On the device itself the app stores essentially only your chosen interface language. Your account and messages live on the server so the service can deliver them.

3. Network requests and third parties (purpose, method, scope)

The app makes network requests to the services below. All requests use HTTPS/TLS where the service supports it. As with any internet request, these recipients necessarily receive your device’s IP address and standard request metadata; this is required to deliver their service and is governed by their own privacy policies. We do not attach your identity to these requests.

4. In-app promotion

The app occasionally shows a full-screen card promoting another app by the same publisher (Dai Grup Ltd. / “Pupikes”). The promoted app is chosen at random from a catalogue we download from our own server — it is not personalised, is not based on any profile of you, and uses no third-party advertising network, no advertising identifier and no tracking SDK. You can close the card immediately.

5. Device permissions

The app requests no special device permissions (no camera, microphone, location, contacts, SMS, call logs or precise location).

6. We do not sell data

The app does not sell personal data to anyone, and does not share it for anyone else’s independent marketing.

7. Sensitive data and children

The app does not use call logs, SMS, contacts, biometrics for identification, health data or precise device location for any purpose beyond what is described above. The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

8. Security

Traffic between the app and our server is encrypted over HTTPS/TLS. We protect account data with standard access controls.

9. Your rights and how to exercise them

You have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data:

  • Access or correct your account data: from within the service, or by emailing us.
  • Delete your account: for security reasons account deletion is not self-service — tap “Delete account” inside the app (or email us) to send a deletion request; only an administrator/moderator performs the deletion. We action verified requests within 30 days and confirm by our support channel.
  • Data held by the third parties listed above is subject to their own policies; you may exercise your rights directly with them.
  • Questions or complaints (feedback channel): email us and we will respond within 30 days.
