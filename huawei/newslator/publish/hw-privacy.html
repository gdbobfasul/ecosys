Privacy Policy — NewsLator

App: NewsLator · Package: com.pupikes.newslator.hw · Store: HUAWEI AppGallery · Provider (data controller): Dai Grup Ltd. · Effective date: 2026-07-11

NewsLator is a news reader. You choose a country/region and NewsLator shows public news for it, and can translate headlines into your language or read them aloud. This policy explains, in plain terms, what data the app processes, why, and with whom it is shared. We do not run user accounts, we do not sell any data, and we do not use advertising or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs.

1. We do not collect personal data on our servers

NewsLator has no registration, no login and no user accounts operated by us. We operate no server that stores information about you. We do not collect, store or transmit to ourselves your name, email, phone number, contacts, precise location, photos or any advertising identifier.

2. Data stored on your device

Your choices — the country/region you select, the interface and translation language, and app settings — are stored only locally on your device. This data never reaches us and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

3. Network requests and third parties (purpose, method, scope)

Because NewsLator fetches live news and can translate it, the app makes network requests to third-party services. All requests use HTTPS/TLS where the service supports it. As with any internet request, these third parties necessarily receive your device's IP address and standard request metadata; this is required to deliver their content and is governed by their own privacy policies. We do not attach your identity to these requests. The third parties are:

We do not disclose personal data to any other party, and we do not use any data for advertising, profiling or personalization.

4. We do not sell data

NewsLator does not sell personal data to anyone, and does not share it for anyone else's independent marketing.

5. Sensitive data, permissions and children

NewsLator does not request the location, camera, microphone, contacts or storage permissions, and does not use call logs, SMS, biometrics, health data or device location for any purpose. The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

6. Security

Our outbound requests to fetch and translate news are made over encrypted HTTPS connections wherever the third-party service offers it. Because there are no accounts and no server operated by us, there is no account database that could be breached.

7. Your rights and how to exercise them

Since we hold no account and store nothing about you on any server, there is no personal data on our side to access, correct, export or delete. You remain in full control on your device:

  • Withdraw consent / stop processing: stop using the translation and news features, or uninstall the app, at any time. No feature is withheld for declining any optional processing.
  • Delete your data: clear the app's storage in your device settings, or uninstall the app — this removes all locally stored choices.
  • Data held by third parties (Google, news outlets, MyMemory) is subject to their own policies linked above; you may exercise your rights directly with them.
  • Questions or complaints (feedback channel): email us and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this same address, with a new effective date.
