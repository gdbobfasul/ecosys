Privacy Policy

App package: com.kcy.chat.rustore · Store: RuStore · Provider: Dai Grup Ltd.

A real-time messaging app that connects you to the KCY chat service. Pick from 15 languages and start chatting once you are online.

1. Personal data

This application connects to an online service. To provide the service, data you enter — including account/registration details and the messages and content you send — is transmitted to and stored on the server, and therefore leaves your device. We do not sell your data or use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

2. Data stored on your device

Your settings and app data (such as the interface language you choose) are stored locally on your device. This data is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Network

The app uses the internet for its stated functions. Connects to a live chat server; messages and account data are sent to and stored on the server, so data leaves the device. The app itself stores only the chosen UI language locally.

4. Children

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.

5. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this address.
