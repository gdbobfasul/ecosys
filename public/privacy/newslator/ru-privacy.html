NewsLator — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026 · App: NewsLator (com.kcy.newslator.rustore) · Store: RuStore · Provider: Dai Grup Ltd.

NewsLator is a news reader that lets you read public news from countries around the world, translated into your language, and read them aloud. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

1. We do not collect personal data

NewsLator has no user accounts, no registration and no login. We do not ask for, collect, store or transmit any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos or precise location. We do not use advertising or third-party analytics / tracking SDKs.

2. Data stored only on your device

Your preferences — the country and language you choose and simple settings (for example whether read-aloud is on) — are stored locally on your device only. They never leave your phone and are removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Network requests

To show news, the app requests publicly available news feeds (RSS) for the country you select. To translate headlines it sends the visible text of those headlines to a translation service. These requests are used only to fetch and translate the news you are reading; no identifier that can identify you personally is attached.

4. Reading aloud

The read-aloud feature uses your device's built-in text-to-speech. The text is processed by the operating system on your device.

5. News sources

News content belongs to its respective publishers. NewsLator is an independent reader and is not affiliated with the news sources it links to.

6. Children

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.
